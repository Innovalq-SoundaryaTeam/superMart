What is Express Delivery?

SuperMart Express Delivery is our flagship same-day grocery service. Using a network of 48 dark stores and our own delivery fleet, we pick, pack, and deliver your groceries in record time — often under 15 minutes.

Whether you've run out of milk at breakfast or need fresh vegetables for dinner, Express Delivery fills the gap without the hassle of a supermarket trip.

What's Included

15-Minute Delivery Promise

Our rider is dispatched the moment your order is confirmed. Live tracking lets you watch every step on the map.

Cold Chain Maintained

Dairy, meat and frozen items are carried in insulated bags to maintain safe temperatures from store to your door.

100% Freshness Guarantee

Not happy with an item? We'll refund or replace it — no questions asked within 24 hours of delivery.

Same Prices as In-Store

No delivery mark-up on products. All weekly offers and discounts apply to Express orders too.

Real-Time Order Support

Chat with our support team inside the app for instant help — item swaps, address changes, and more.

How It Works

1

4

Your rider arrives — track them live. Leave delivery instructions (e.g. "Leave at door") in the app.
